Kristen Lee Calligraphy and Fine Art is the professional work of Boston based artist Kristen Lee, specializing in classic and modern calligraphy and hand lettering, photo realism portrait art as well as a handful of other various artistic mediums.


Eschewing any single style, Kristen's design philosophy is based upon the order and proportion of timeless design principles. She is a lifelong student and self taught artist and calligrapher, who brings 20+ years of artistic experience and knowledge to her work.


Offering a variety of services, Kristen welcomes you to her site and encourages you to view her work, and fill out the contact form for questions, pricing, custom design or collaborations.

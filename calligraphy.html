ENVELOPES - Hand addressed envelopes for your special event never go unnoticed. In this age of emails and texts a calligraphed envelope feels personal and special, and is often saved as a memento for your special occasion.

SPOT CALLIGRAPHY - A less expensive alternative than hiring a calligrapher to create your whole piece (invitation, quote, etc). The client picks certain important elements of the piece to be calligraphed and paired with a computer generated font.


PLACE CARDS - traditionally, this was a small folded card placed on the table indicating the guest’s seat. Today people are getting really creative with the materials they are using as well as the formats.

ESCORT CARDS - typically arranged at the entrance of the reception, these are meant to guide the guest to their table. These can be a creative alternative to a seating chart and give your guests a small meaningful keepsake of a beautiful day.

PROGRAMS - may be as simple as just a card or a more extravagant handmade booklet and can be left for guests to read while waiting on the start of the ceremony. Gives the couple an opportunity to include fun info about yourselves, your bridal party, or maybe info about how the night will carry on.


MENUS - a personalized menu can be used to substitute a place card and it’s a great great way to showcase and commemorate the amazing meal to come.


SINAGE - seating charts, welcome signs and station signs are just a couple of ways to incorporate calligraphy into your event. These are small details that are as practical as they are charming and memorable
